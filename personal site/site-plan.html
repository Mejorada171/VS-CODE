Overview

Purpose

This web page has the purpose of receive all the coustumers and potential coustumers to show all the tools they may need, Tool Market is a new company trying to get in the way of the big ones, so they're looking impact all the people, this page would show products (tools) of all kinds, prices, characteristics and brands, also would have useful information or tips about working with tools, finally would be a section where they can find the contact information or maybe send us their requests. This website would be structured by a principal web page and at least 3 child pages, one for tips about construction, another for discounts and promotions and the last one for contact information.

Audience

Our target coustumers are young people and people no so young with thirst for adventure, people around the 20 and 40 with the economic resource to buy a plan of rafting, these people are business owners, professionals and investors which usually have great quantity of activities day by day, but now they want a refreshing and unique experience to get away from that world for a while No! They're not seeking for another ordinary sea vacation or a simple pool in a hotel surrounded of buildings, cars, pollution and the same stress of the suburbs, what they want is a paceful place with unique activities and unforgottable experiences, being with family or friends feeling some of the risk of rafting through the White Waters. They'll reach our web page by laptop but in the most of the times by mobile device.
