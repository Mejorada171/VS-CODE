Audience

Our target coustumers are companies of constructions of all the sizes, solo workers, entrepreneurs in the construction field and people who just want some small tools to be able to do some chores at home on weekends. This web site would show tips and information about working with tools, so maybe some new-born hard workers would come to learn more about construction.
